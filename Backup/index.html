About Me

I'm Shubham Upreti, a versatile artist, flutist, blogger, and tutor. Through music, I express emotions; with words, I paint vivid thoughts.
As a flutist, I breathe life into melodies, sharing my passion on Youtube through recordings. My blog is a canvas for exploring diverse topics, inviting you to see the world through my words.
I'm also the founder of the Student Alliance For Creation, a community nurturing artistic talents and showcasing their creativity. We believe in the power of collective inspiration.
As Science, Mathematics and English tutor, I help others master the subject and clear their doubts. I am also actively engaged in writing articles and educational blogs.
